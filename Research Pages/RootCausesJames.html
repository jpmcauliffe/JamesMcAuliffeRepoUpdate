Lowering the cost of organized sports will increase the participation of children because families will not have to stop their children from playing due to cost reasons.

  • The main limiting factor at the moment is high cost, decreasing the cost decreases the effect of this limitation
  • This would benefit those of low SES, who tend to need the organized programs the most

Technology can be used to create cost-effectiveness and diversification algorithms, which will help lower the cost of organized programs and include as many interested people as possible.
  • Algorithms can be created and run for statistical analysis of racial and economic diversity within organized sports.
  • Algorithms to determine cost-effectiveness can help reduce costs across the board.

High costs disproportionately affect minorities
  • This leads to under-involvement by minorities
    • Which further leads to heightened inactivity rates
      • Meaning increased chances of obesity, among other physical and mental health issues for minorities
    • This creates a lack of diversity within these sports programs
    • This also prevents lots of talent from reaching the biggest stage
